Ice climbing & hiking on glaciers

Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an esay glacier hike on Sólhemiajökull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at Sólhemiajökull glacier. Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us...

Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an esay glacier hike on Sólhemiajökull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at Sólhemiajökull glacier. Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us...

Sólhemiajökull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Mýyrdalsjõkull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanoes and during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land!
